About Vitur

Expert IT and cyber security services across the Central Belt of Scotland, based in Bathgate.

Vitur Ltd is an IT, cloud and cyber security services company serving the Central Belt of Scotland, with operations based in Bathgate. We help businesses across Edinburgh, Glasgow, Falkirk, Stirling and all Lothian regions modernise their IT systems, improve reliability, reduce cloud costs and strengthen security. With expertise in cloud platforms, DevOps, infrastructure automation and cyber security, we deliver practical, cost-effective solutions tailored to your business needs.

  • Cloud cost optimisation and management across all platforms
  • DevOps consultancy, CI/CD automation and infrastructure as code
  • Cyber security reviews, hardening and compliance (GDPR, ISO 27001)
  • IT infrastructure setup, migration support and technical management
  • General IT support and troubleshooting for SMEs
  • Practical solutions tailored to Central Belt Scotland businesses
